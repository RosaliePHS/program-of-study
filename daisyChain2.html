9 simple rules for dressing when fat
by Miriam Zisook
Nobody wants to look fat, but there are so many myths out there about how to dress your best. Here we wade through the myths to share 9 tried and true tips for dressing to fit and flatter. Look 10 pounds thinner by tonight if you follow these wardrobe tricks.
1. Streamline With (mono) Color, especially black
Glamour: How to Dress 10 Pounds Thinner
"Look taller and trimmer by dressing monochromatically head to toe. Match your separates: "Lose" five pounds by matching your pants or skirt, stockings and shoes. You'll seem 10 pounds lighter if they're all black! Bright colors and prints will call unnecessary attention to your shape and make you look bigger. Is the one-color thing too tame for your taste? Slip on a pair of bright shoes to shake things up."
