--- title: The Joan Didion thing about the idea baby vs the real baby dateCreated: '2018-10-31 14:14:31 +0000' dateUpdated: '2018-11-21 14:55:30 +0000' author: Miriam Zisook ---
There’s a thing Joan Didion said about grieving a bay that died, and how that baby will always be perfect because it never had time to disappoint her. I am interested in that idea, and what it might mean about perfection—namely that it’s completely imaginary, and every moment of being a human being is about falling further away from it.
